# Project Summary

## Overview
End-to-end data analytics project analyzing BMW global vehicle sales
from 2018 to 2025 across 4 regions and 8 models.

## Objective
Help BMW management understand sales performance, regional demand,
pricing impact, and EV adoption trends to support future market
strategy and EV expansion decisions.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel — data cleaning and analysis
- Power BI Desktop — dashboard and visualization
- DAX — KPI measures and calculations

## Workflow
Raw Data → Clean Data → Sales Analysis → EV Analysis →
Economic Analysis → Insights → Recommendations → Power BI Dashboard

## Key Numbers
- 3,000+ rows cleaned and analyzed
- 4 regions covered
- 8 BMW models analyzed
- 8 years of data (2018–2025)
- 2 Power BI dashboard pages
- 5 business insights delivered
- 5 strategic recommendations made

## Top 3 Findings
1. BMW sales grew 21.7% from 2.76M to 3.36M units (2018–2025)
2. BEV share grew 9.4x from 2.07% to 19.43% (2018–2025)
3. Fuel price index is the strongest driver of EV adoption —
   near-perfect positive correlation across all 8 years
